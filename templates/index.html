Auto Topic Classification

Use this prototype as a way to quickly start any new Topic Classification Project.
All you need to do is just upload your dataset in csv format, sit back, have a cup of coffee and relax

Steps to use this project:

  1. Sign up into the application or if you already have an account please login with your credentials.
  2. Click on 'New Project' available on the sidebar of the dashboard.
  3. Name your project and later upload a dataset in CSV format ONLY. The dataset should contain two columns "data" on which your model should be trained and "class" which is the class of the text present in the data coulumn.
  4. Click on the train button and wait for your data to be pre-processed and trained on different models. After training, a model with best accuracy will be saved.
  5. To test or use your trained model, click on "My Projects" available on the sidebar of the dashboard. Select your project from the available projects and enter your text for prediction.
